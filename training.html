Conduct Atmospheric Monitoring Program

Course overview:

This training will provide the skills and knowledge required to identify and monitor gases and other airborne contaminants in mining operational areas and confined spaces.

The program is delivered by specialist QMRS trainers at Blackwater and Dysart Rescue Facilities. If requested the course can be delivered at other locations with approval of QMRS and availability of resources.

This training covers the requirements for the Opencut Examiners Certificate of Proficiency in Mine Gases and Gas Testing.

Course numbers are minimum of 4 to maximum of 8 students. QMRS reserve the right to cancel or reschedule a course if the minimum numbers are not attained prior to the training commencing. QMRS refund policy is covered in the enrolment form.

Training competencies covered in the course:

  • RIIENV301E Conduct Atmospheric Monitoring

RIIENV301E Course duration:

2 days, 07:00 hr - 16:00 hr

Underground Coal Mine Emergency Response and Rescue Team Training Program

This training will provide you with skills and knowledge to work effectively as a member of an Underground Coal Mine Emergency Response and Rescue Team. Training is delivered by specialist QMRS trainers at Blackwater and Dysart Rescue stations. If approved resources are available, units of competency within the program can also be trained at an underground minesite.

The program covers the following units of competency from 10876NAT Certificate III in Underground Coal Mines Emergency Response and Rescue:

  • RIIERR201E Conduct fire team operations
  • RIIERR302E Respond to local emergencies and incidents
  • RIIERR303E Operate in self-contained regenerative oxygen breathing apparatus
  • RIIERR305E Control underground fires
  • RIIERR306E Conduct underground searches
  • RIIERR307E Extricate casualties from underground incidents
  • RIIERR309D Establish and operate from fresh air base
  • PUAFIR207 Operate breathing apparatus open circuit
  • PUAFIR210 Prevent injury
  • PUATEA001 Work in a team
  • NAT10876001 Detect and sample underground coal mine atmosphere
  • NAT10876002 Operate oxygen resuscitation breathing apparatus in underground coal mine emergency response
  • NAT10876003 Operate self-contained self-rescuer
  • NAT10876004 Survey and monitor underground coal mine environment

Mine Emergency Preparedness and Response Systems

This training program provides you with the skills and knowledge required to establish and maintain mine emergency preparedness and response systems and establish post-incident management procedures.

The courses focus on either underground or surface mining operations. It can be delivered at QMRS facilities or onsite as required. The applicable unit of competency for:

  • Surface coal mines, metalliferous surface mines and metalliferous underground mines is RIIERR601E Establish and maintain mine emergency preparedness and response systems.
  • Underground coal mines is RIIERR602E Establish and maintain underground coal mine emergency preparedness and response systems.
Mine Event Management System (MEMS) Training Program

The MEMS training program provides you with the skills and knowledge to organise and manage a structured and efficient response to an emergency situation at a coal mine.

The MEMS course can be contextualised to focus on either underground or surface mining operations. It is delivered at QMRS facilities or onsite as required. The applicable unit of competency for:

  • underground operations is RIIERR501E Implement underground coal mine emergency preparedness and response systems
  • surface operations is RIIERR503D Implement emergency preparedness and response systems.

This course also gains 15 hours for the professional development requirement of the RSHQ Practising Certificate Scheme.

Underground Coal Mine Control Room Operator Program

This program will provide you with the skills and knowledge to work effectively as an underground coal mine control room operator. The program is predominantly delivered on site, provided suitable resources are available.

This training covers the following units of competency from RII30220 Certificate III in Underground Coal Operations:

  • RIIMCU311E Monitor control processes
  • RIIGOV201E Comply with site work processes/procedures.
